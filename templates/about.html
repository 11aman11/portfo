Welcome To My Journey

Greetings! I'm Aman Singh, an enthusiastic Software Engineering Co-op student at Concordia University. Fueled by a deep interest in technology, I have learned and applied a range of programming languages such as C++, Java, Python, among others, in both complex academic and personal projects.

My journey is a blend of rigorous education and proactive self-learning. I've augmented my university curriculum by diving into frameworks and tools like Node.js, React, and MongoDB, sharpening my full-stack development acumen. My hands-on experience at Bombardier as a Business Information Solutions Intern allowed me to innovate at the intersection of software and enterprise systems, enhancing decision-making and operational efficiency through data analytics.

I've manifested my passion for technology through a portfolio of dynamic projects, from conceptualizing a real estate platform to deploying my own full-stack website, each project a testament to my analytical prowess and managerial skills. My personal commitment to continuous growth is exemplified by a stellar GPA of 3.99, membership in the Golden Key International Honour Society, and a proactive role in various extracurricular activities.

As I navigate the challenges of academia and beyond, I remain committed to excellence, balancing my academic responsibilities with a fervor for practical, hands-on experiences. Join me as I continue to forge my path in the tech world, innovating, learning, and connecting dots between technology and its real-world applications.
